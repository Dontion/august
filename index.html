About

Teratai Putih Global II Vocational High School

Teratai Putih Global II Vocational High School have accredited "A"

Teratai Putih Global 2 Bekasi Vocational School is one of the Vocational High Schools established by the Bekasi Global Teratai Foundation. Like SMKs in general in Indonesia, the school education period at Teratai Putih Global 2 Bekasi Vocational School is taken within three academic years, starting from Class X to Class XII.

Club

What is Club?


Club activities are additional activities carried out outside of school hours that are carried out either at school or outside of school with the aim of gaining additional knowledge, skills and insights and helping to shape the character of students according to their individual interests and talents.

Sports

Basketball

Basketball is a game played between two teams of five players each on a rectangular court, usually indoors. Each team tries to score by tossing the ball through the opponent's goal, an elevated horizontal hoop and net called a basket.

Futsal

Futsal is a football-(soccer) based game played on a hard court like a basketball court, smaller than a football pitch, and mainly indoors. It has similarities to five-a-side football and indoor football

Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport in which two teams of six players are separated by a net. Each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court under organized rules.

Sains

English Club

An English Club is a place for language learners to use English in a casual setting. Practising your skills in the classroom is important, but it is not like real life. In the classroom, you often focus on one skill and one item

Scout

Scouting, also known as the Scout Movement, is a worldwide youth movement employing the Scout method, a program of informal education with an emphasis on practical outdoor activities, including camping, woodcraft, aquatics, hiking, backpacking, and sports.

Study

What is meant by study?


The department is part of the faculty, namely the focus of the field of study chosen and studied by students. Disciplines in a study are usually divided into three parts, namely humanities, exact sciences, and social sciences. You need to know, the terms study program have the same meaning.

RPL

The Software Engineering Expertise Program (RPL) is one of the competency skills in the field of Computer Technology and Informatics that specifically studies computer programming.

MM

The Multimedia major focuses on utilizing technology to produce and distribute multimedia content. Multimedia is basically divided into two types, namely linear and interactive

BDP

Online Business and Marketing is an expertise competence (BDP) that studies the basic skills and knowledge of becoming a marketing professional, both conventional marketing and through online media (online/internet).

AKL

Institutional Financial Accounting (AKL) is a department that studies recording methods, classification, and preparation of reports both manually and computerized as well as tax calculations

OTKP

The Department of Office Automation and Management (OTKP) or formerly Office Administration (AP) is one of the majors at the Vocational High School (SMK) which provides provision with knowledge, skills and attitudes in completing company or office jobs.

PBK

The Department of Finance and Banking is a study that studies everything about banking and finance. Students majoring in this study learn about the financial system and operational systems that exist in financial institutions, especially banking, both conventional banks and Islamic banks.
